    • Heritage Day - 24th September
    On this day, we recognise and celebrate the cultural wealth of our nation.
    Various Events are staged throughout the country to commemorate this day and
    at Mzamomtsha Primary School, we join in on the festivities.





    Valentines Day - 14th February
    An annual celebrate of love, friendship, and admiration,
    yearly people spend this day sending messages of love and affection to
    partners, family members and, sometimes, friends as well.
    At Mzamomtsha we hold a civvies day for the students, allowing them to dress up
    in the signature colors of the day and let them send each other messages of
    affection to their friends, educators and parents.





    Career Day - TBA
    We understand the importance of having dreams at our school, so once, every year,
    we have a day allowing our students to dress up in the clothes of the career
    they may wish to pursue as an adult. Through this,
    we can create a sense of self-motivation for students as we allow them to see
    what they are actually striving towards doing in the future.
    Held every year on different days.





    Market Day - TBA

    A fun-filled day where the grade 7 class uses the opportunity to raise funds
    for the school for things like their end of year prom.
    While they do require a lot of preparation, organisation and energy, the common goal is
    to instill a sense of awareness when it comes to money, its management and it also promotes team work.
    Held every year on different days.
